    Clove

    Rich aroma, used in cooking and Ayurveda. Cloves are aromatic flower buds used worldwide for their strong flavor and medicinal properties. They enhance curries, baked goods, and herbal teas, while also being valued in traditional Ayurveda.

    Cinnamon

    Sweet-spicy flavor, perfect for desserts & curries. Cinnamon is a sweet-spicy bark spice native to Sri Lanka. It is widely used in desserts, curries, and beverages. Renowned for its health benefits, it helps regulate blood sugar and boosts immunity.

    Black Pepper

    Known as the 'King of Spices', adds strong flavor. Known as the "King of Spices," pepper is prized for its sharp, pungent flavor. It elevates savory dishes, improves digestion, and is a key export of Sri Lanka's spice heritage.
